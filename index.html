Scroll #1: I Stayed Too Long

I never wanted to be too much. I just didn’t want to be hurt again. Do you know what that ache really feels like? To be lying next to someone every day… and still feel invisible? To give your heart, your body, your prayers — and still not be chosen?

This scroll is for the women who stayed too long, hoping love would remember them. It’s for the girls who never learned how to leave — because nobody taught them that saving yourself was holy.
